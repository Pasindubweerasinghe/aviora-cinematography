About

A Frame is Never
Just a Frame

I'm a cinematographer based in Colombo, Sri Lanka — specializing in narrative-driven visuals for film, commercial, and documentary work.

Every shot is built around the story it needs to tell. Whether it's the chaos of a handheld chase or the stillness of a single-source lit portrait, I believe light and composition are the first language of cinema.

How I See

01

Natural Light

Working with the sun, shade, and practical sources to create organic, textured frames.

02

Anamorphic

Wide aspect ratios, lens flares, and the distinctive oval bokeh of anamorphic glass.

03

Color Design

Intentional color palettes — built on set, refined in grade — that serve the story's emotional core.

04

Handheld

Visceral, intimate camera movement that puts the viewer inside the scene, not watching from outside it.
